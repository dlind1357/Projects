Welcome to Treasure Hunt!

You are stuck on an island and your mission is to collect all the treasure while avoiding the landmines. Luckily for you, you have a very handy metal detector that can help you detect when a landmine is near.

To start your mission, click on "Warm Up" or "Start Game" in the Navigation Bar above which redirect you to the screen below and automatically start the game.

Click anywhere to start looking for treasure.

If you click on a tile and see a number, that number represents how many landmines are near that tile.

You can use the ? in order to mark unrevealed tiles.

If you find a treasure, the tile will turn light blue.

If you click on a landmine, you fail your mission and it's game over.



Now that you know the rules of the game, GOOD LUCK!
